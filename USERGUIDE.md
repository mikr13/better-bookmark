# Recall It - User Guide

## Getting Started

### Installation

1. Download and install the extension from the browser store (Chrome Web Store or Firefox Add-ons)
2. Click on the Recall It icon in your browser toolbar
3. You're ready to start building your knowledge graph!

## Features

### 1. Saving Pages

There are two ways to save a page:

**Method 1: Extension Popup**
- Click the Recall It icon in your browser toolbar
- Click the "Save Page" button
- The current page will be saved with its keywords

**Method 2: Right-Click Menu**
- Right-click anywhere on the page
- Select "Save page to Recall It"
- The page will be saved instantly

When you save a page, the extension:
- Extracts the page title and main content
- Identifies the most important keywords (10-15 keywords)
- Automatically categorizes the content
- Creates a short excerpt for quick reference
- Stores everything locally in your browser

### 2. Browsing Saved Cards

Click the extension icon and navigate to the "Cards" tab:

- **Search**: Type in the search box to find cards by title or keyword
- **Filter by Category**: Click on category chips to filter cards
- **View Details**: Click on a card title to open the original page
- **Delete**: Click the "Delete" button to remove a card

### 3. Keyword Highlighting

This is where the magic happens! As you browse the web:

- Keywords from your saved cards are **automatically highlighted**
- Highlights use a subtle translucent background and underline
- The highlighting adapts to light/dark mode automatically
- **Click on any highlighted keyword** to see a popup with related cards
- Click on a card in the popup to open it

#### Highlighting Behavior

- Only meaningful keywords are highlighted (no common words)
- Highlights update when you save new pages
- Works on any website you visit
- Non-intrusive design that doesn't disrupt reading

### 4. Statistics

Navigate to the "Stats" tab to see:

- **Total Cards**: Number of pages you've saved
- **Keywords Tracked**: Unique keywords across all cards
- **Connections**: Number of connections between cards (based on shared keywords)
- **Category Breakdown**: How your cards are distributed across categories

### 5. Knowledge Graph

Navigate to the "Knowledge Graph" tab to explore:

- Visual representation of connections between your saved cards
- Top keywords and how many cards they appear in
- Understanding how your knowledge is interconnected

## Categories

The extension automatically categorizes pages into:

- **Technology**: Programming, software, APIs, development
- **Science**: Research, studies, scientific topics
- **Business**: Markets, companies, startups, strategy
- **News**: Current events, announcements, updates
- **General**: Everything else

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Save Quality Content**: The extension works best with content-rich pages
2. **Regular Saving**: Save pages as you research to build your knowledge graph over time
3. **Use Keywords**: Keywords are the foundation - the more pages you save, the better the highlighting
4. **Click Highlights**: Don't forget to click highlighted keywords to discover connections
5. **Clean Up**: Periodically delete cards you no longer need

## Privacy & Data

- ✅ All data is stored **locally in your browser**
- ✅ No data is sent to external servers
- ✅ No tracking or analytics
- ✅ No internet connection needed after installation
- ✅ Your data stays with you

## Troubleshooting

### Keywords aren't being highlighted

1. Make sure you have saved at least one page
2. Wait a moment after the page loads for highlighting to apply
3. Some dynamic websites may need a refresh after saving a card

### Highlights look wrong on certain websites

The extension tries to detect light/dark mode automatically. If it doesn't look right, it might be due to custom website styling.

### Extension not working on certain pages

Some pages (like browser settings pages, chrome:// URLs, or extension pages) cannot have content scripts injected for security reasons.

### Data not syncing between devices

The extension stores data locally in each browser. There is no built-in sync feature to maintain privacy.

## Keyboard Shortcuts

Currently, the extension doesn't have keyboard shortcuts, but you can set them up in your browser:

- **Chrome**: Go to `chrome://extensions/shortcuts`
- **Firefox**: Go to `about:addons`, click the gear icon, select "Manage Extension Shortcuts"
